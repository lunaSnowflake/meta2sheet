Meta2Sheet

Meta2Sheet is a Chrome Extension that allows users to transfer data from Metabase dashboards directly into their Google Sheets.

Features

  • Securely authenticate with Google Sheets using OAuth2
  • Extract visible dashboard filters from Metabase
  • Write dashboard data to a chosen sheet and range
  • Overwrite or append modes for Google Sheets

Privacy

This extension does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. All processing occurs locally or through authorized calls to Google APIs. View our full Privacy Policy.
